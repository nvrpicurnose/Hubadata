Order Placement & Queues

With the Petramid Smartphone app, users can place orders directly from their phone at anyplace, anytime. When it's ready, they'll be able to pick it up on the way home.


Our new queue system also makes it easy for you as a business owner to organize incoming requests. Orders placed through Petramid are automatically filed to make your job simplier, cleaner and more effective.
